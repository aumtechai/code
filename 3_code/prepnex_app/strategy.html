PrepAI: Your Personalized College Pathfinder (6th–12th Grade)

Unified Ecosystem
aumtech.ai/prepai

Pros

  • Feeder System: PrepAI acts as a 7-year "onramp" (6th-12th) for the Student Success platform.
  • Data Continuity: Academic courses planned in PrepAI seamlessly import into Aura for college degree tracking.
  • Single Subscription: A "Family Plan" could cover both high-school prep and college success.

Cons

  • Market Positioning: Middle school parents may find a "College Success" platform's UI too complex.

Subdomain Approach
prepai.aumtech.ai

Pros

  • Targeted Pillars: Focus 100% on the 4 Core Pillars (Academic Engine, Profile Builder, Testing, Coaching).
  • Elite Branding: Standalone feel for "Elite Higher Education" focus.

Cons

  • Integrity: Must ensure seamless data handoff when the student hits 12th grade graduation.

1. The Academic Engine

Automated 6th–12th-grade math and science course planning aligned with specific university major requirements.

2. Holistic Profile Builder

AI-suggested volunteering, internships, and co-curriculars (Robotics, Olympiads) tailored to the target major.

3. Testing & Strategy

Dynamic SAT/ACT prep timelines and score tracking to ensure peak performance for elite admissions.

4. Human-in-the-Loop

Tiered access to virtual and in-person coaching sessions to complement AI guidance with human expertise.

Recommended Path

The Subdomain Hybrid

Utilize prepai.aumtech.ai as the specialized portal for 6th–12th graders. This maintains brand authority while providing a laser-focused environment for elite higher education prep.
