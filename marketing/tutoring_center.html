📊 Course-Related Grades

  • Provide the current course-related grades (cumulative across all tests, quizzes, etc.).

🚦 GPA Status Indicator

Provide a Red / Amber / Green status to indicate the level of successful progress.

🟢 Green

Grade A & Above – On track

🟡 Amber

Grade B–C – Needs attention

🔴 Red

Grade D & Below – At risk

📋 Status-Based List

  • Also list courses sorted by status indicator (Red listed first).
  • This will help the student prioritize and decide ADD/DROP actions.

➕➖ ADD/DROP Course

  • Form appears on the Dashboard (denoted as <CHG>).
  • List courses based on GPA Status Indicator to help determine what to DROP.
  • List alternate courses available in a future semester if Red in a course.
